I play ultimate frisbee for the University of South Carolina. The last 4 years I've been a player on the B team. Last year the B team won the Atlantic Coast Dev Regionals tournament. This is the end of year tournament against all the other B teams across the East Coast. I happened to score 5 and throw 2 of my teams 15 goals in the championship game.

    What you need to play frisbee
  • Cleats
  • A Frisbee. A 175 gram Ultrastar is the specific weight and model of frisbee that we play with.
  • A field. The field we play on is 120 yards long, and 40 yards wide. There are 20 yard endzones on each side of the field.
